## 2. Expected Behaviour

All participants are expected to:

- Respect perspectives that differ from their own.
- Provide and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Take responsibility for their words and actions.
- Apologize and correct behaviour when mistakes occur.
- Use professional, inclusive language.
- Act in the best interest of the Project and its community.

## 4. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All Project repositories, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and documentation.

- Activities where individuals represent the Project, publicly or privately.

## 5. Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers and designated moderators are responsible for:

- Reviewing, moderating, and guiding community behaviour.
- Removing or editing inappropriate contributions.
- Issuing warnings, restrictions, or bans when necessary.
- Ensuring enforcement is applied fairly and consistently.

Maintainers themselves must uphold this Code as examples for the community.
